The Secret History

Author: Donna Tartt

Genre: Thriller

Year Released: 1992

Weeks on the New York Times bestseller list: 13
Fun Fact: The Secret History is widely considered to be the book that launched the "Dark Academia" aesthetic.

The Secret History is a novel about six classics students at a small elite college in Vermont.
It is an inverted detective story narratated by one of the students, Richard Papen.
The book discusses the events that led to the murder of one of the students, and how the death affected the others.

Characters

  • Richard Papen
  • Henry Winter
  • Camilla Macaulay
  • Charles Macaulay

  • Edmund "Bunny" Corcoran
